Created in ~72 hours by Chris Logsdon and Jacob Albano
Explore the caves to find missing mementos and bring them back home.
Avoid exposure and water or risk freezing to death. Rest near campfires to warm up.
Escape from the cave through the shaft at the top of the map.

Controls:

  • A, D to move left and right.
  • Space to jump. Space while in midair to jetpack.
